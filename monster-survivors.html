About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an exhilarating roguelike survival game that challenges you to fend off relentless hordes of monsters while growing stronger with each passing moment. Inspired by the wildly popular survival genre, this game combines fast-paced action with strategic depth, creating an experience that's easy to pick up but endlessly replayable.

In Monster Survivors, your character automatically attacks nearby enemies, allowing you to focus on movement and positioning. As you defeat monsters, you'll collect experience gems that level you up and unlock powerful abilities. The game features multiple playable characters, each with unique starting abilities and playstyles. Frequently Asked Questions

Is Monster Survivors free to play?

Yes! Monster Survivors is completely free to play. No downloads, no registrations, no hidden fees. Just click play and start surviving.

Can I play on my phone or tablet?

Absolutely! Monster Survivors is fully optimized for mobile devices. The game features touch controls with a virtual joystick for easy navigation on touchscreens.

New characters are unlocked by achieving specific milestones in the game, such as surviving for a certain amount of time, defeating a number of monsters, or reaching particular levels.
